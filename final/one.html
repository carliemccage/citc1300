The Perfect Proposal... or Not

Elle Woods is the queen bee of Delta Nu, stylish, brilliant in her own way, and head-over-heels for Warner Huntington III. She’s expecting an engagement ring—but gets heartbreak instead when Warner breaks up with her, saying he needs someone more “serious” for his future in politics.

"If I'm gonna be a senator, I need to marry a Jackie, not a Marilyn."

After Elle Woods was dumped by her boyfriend Warner, her world was shaken—but only for a moment. Devastated yet determined, Elle transformed heartbreak into motivation. Rather than wallow in rejection, she boldly chased Warner to Harvard Law School, not to win him back, but to prove her worth.

What Elle did to win over Harvard
