The Story of Elle Woods

Elle Woods’ story is a glittery, bold tale of transformation, confidence, and proving people wrong with kindness and smarts.

Elle gets dumped

It all starts with Elle as a stylish, bubbly sorority girl at UCLA, living her best life and dreaming of marrying her preppy boyfriend, Warner. But when Warner dumps her for not being "serious" enough before heading off to Harvard Law, Elle is heartbroken—but not defeated. Determined to win him back, she studies hard, nails the LSATs, and gets into Harvard herself (in a very Elle Woods kind of way).
